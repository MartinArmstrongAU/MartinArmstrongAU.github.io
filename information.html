Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I trigger an emergency alert?

You can trigger an emergency alert through our app or by using a connected wearable device, such as a smartwatch, for immediate action.

What types of emergencies can the drone respond to?

Our drones can assist in various emergency situations, such as medical distress, natural disasters (fires, floods), or accidents where immediate help is needed.

What kind of equipment does the drone carry?

The drones are equipped with medical kits, defibrillators (AEDs), first aid supplies, and real-time video streaming capabilities for emergency responders to assess the situation.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we prioritize your privacy. All your personal and medical information is securely stored and handled according to industry best practices.

Safety Protocols

We take safety seriously and follow strict protocols to ensure the effectiveness and reliability of our service:

  • All drones are equipped with GPS tracking for precise location accuracy.
  • Real-time video streaming allows emergency responders to make informed decisions and prepare appropriate interventions.
  • Medical supplies onboard are carefully curated to cater to common emergencies, including CPR kits, AEDs, and first aid supplies.
  • Our service is available 24/7 in the most affected areas, ensuring help is always within reach.
